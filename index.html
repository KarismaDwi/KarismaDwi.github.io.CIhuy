Popular Recreational Activities New

Budapest - The Jewel of the Danube

Explore the stunning capital city with iconic landmarks like the Buda Castle, Parliament Building, Chain Bridge, and the charming Fisherman's Bastion. Relax in one of Budapest's famous thermal baths, such as Széchenyi or Gellért Fürdő.

More
Lake Balaton - The Hungarian Sea

Visit Lake Balaton, the largest freshwater lake in Central Europe. Enjoy water sports, cycling around the lake, or relaxing on the beach. Don’t miss the quaint villages of Tihany and Badacsony, known for their vineyards and scenic views.

More
Eger - Wine and Baroque Architecture

Discover the town of Eger, famous for its baroque buildings and Eger Castle. Wine enthusiasts can explore the Szépasszonyvölgy (Valley of the Beautiful Women) for wine tasting, especially the celebrated Egri Bikavér (Bull’s Blood wine).
